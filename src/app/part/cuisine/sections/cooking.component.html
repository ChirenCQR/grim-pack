A few of the food items that you make are used to make some tasty treats and meals. The food can include basic cheese or butter, but can also end up being a delicious sandwich or apple pie.

Utensils

Probably the first the thing that you will want to do is create some utensils to start cooking. As of right now there is only two utensils, a knife and mixer. But, if you like pie you might also want to make the Pie pan.

After you have some utensils you might want to make some of the blocks used to make the adairy products. These include a cheese maker and a butter churn.

Cheese Maker

The Cheese Maker transforms Milk into Cheese after a while. So you put Milk in it, wait some time and it’ll transform into a blocky cheese. Let’s get started: Recipe: 7 Cobblestone and 1 bucket.

Cheese Block

Recipe: 9 pieces of cheese. This will be the final product of the Cheese Maker. Is placeable like cake, but you can’t eat it.

Using the CheeseMaker

  1. Empty a bucket of milk into the cheese maker.
  2. Wait some time, go hunt, mine, anything.
  3. When it’s ready, Left-click the maker to release the cheese.
  4. Storage, slice into pieces, do anything you want with it.

Butter Churn

With this, you can transform one bucket of milk into two pieces of butter. Let’s get started: Recipe: 7 planks and 2 sticks.

Butter

Recipe: Not craftable. This is what you get from the Butter Churn: two pieces of butter.They ARE stackable.

Using the Churn

  1. Place the Churn.
  2. Place a bucket of milk in your hand.
  3. Empty the bucket inside the churn.
  4. Left-Click the Churn to release the Butter.
  5. Sell on ebay.
  6. ????
  7. Profit.

Eggs

Now we that we have most of the tools and basic ingredients we can start making some food. To start off with we can cook some eggs. First you will need to pour the eggs into a bowl.

Then you will need to mix the eggs and finally cook them to perfection. So put them in a furnace.

Sandwiches

Maybe eggs aren't your thing and you want something tastier. You might want to make some sandwiches. Currently the only sandwiches you can make is a cheese burger and a grilled cheese.

Bread Slice

Before you can make the sandwiches you will first need some bread slices. Now you can make the sandwiches. The sandwiches both use cheese that you can make from the cheese maker above.

Pies

Now maybe you want some dessert so now you can make some Pie. You have already made your Pie pan so the first thing you will need is a raw pie without any filling. Now you can start filling up your pie with whatever filling you want. Also the chocolate pie uses Chocolate from Grim Pack.

Finally just cook the pie and you can eat it like vanilla cake
