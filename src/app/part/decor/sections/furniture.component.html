Decor adds in a wide variety of furniture to your world. Each of these can be colored to look like many different blocks. These blocks all function the same way as basic colorizer blocks.

Tables

These tables can be used as a good decoration and will attach to other tables that they are connected to.


Chairs

Currently chairs are only for decoration. You can't sit or anything at the moment but it is a planned feature. Chairs can be placed in all four different directions.


Stools

Stools are small seats that again are decoration only at the moment. Stools will change shape if a Clay Pot is on them.


Walls

These walls act as vanilla walls but with the ability to be colorized.


Doors

These are like vanilla doors except can be colorized.


Lamp Posts

These are 3 block high lamp posts that emit light from the top block. They are useful for street lamps and look very nice.


Counters

These are basically the top half of a table that floats. It doesn't really have much of a use other than decoration.


Fences

These work just like vanilla fences except they can be colorized.


Fence Gates

These work just like vanilla fence gates except they can be colorized.
