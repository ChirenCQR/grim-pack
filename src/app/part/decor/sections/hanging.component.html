Decor also adds in many ways to make your walls more interesting and pleasing.


Calendar

The calendar takes the time of the world and spits out the date in Gregorian format on a wall calendar.

Works like a sign except you can only stick it to walls.


Wall Clock

Basically it’s the everyday watch, but placed on the wall.

Recipe: 4 planks, 4 gold ingots and one redstone dust. OR 8 planks, already made clock at the center. You can turn the wall clock on clock again by putting it on the crafting square. It’s the wall clock!



Flat Item Frame

The flat item frames are like the vanilla item frame except that you can put them on the ground and underneath blocks.



Wallpaper

Wallpaper can be placed on your walls as a decorative texture. This way, you can have two different faces of a block decorated with two different texture and customize the bedrooms of your house without having to add new blocks. It is recommended to combine wallpaper with Frames for door and window frames.

It is possible to change the texture by right-clicking on the wallpaper while having another wallpaper in your hand.

When you will place another wallpaper close to an existing one, it will automatically take the same texture.

You can still change a texture by right-clicking it anytime you want.

If you have some dye, you can then color a texture by right-clicking it with dye equipped.

If you want to remove a wallpaper, you need to have the item in your hand or an axe. It is possible to combine the wallpaper with any item on the wall: Torch, Painting…
